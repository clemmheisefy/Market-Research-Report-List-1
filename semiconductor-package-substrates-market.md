<p><p>The semiconductor package substrates market includes various types catering to different applications. MCP/UTCSP (Multi-Chip Package/Ultra Thin Chip Scale Package) facilitates compact designs; FC-CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package) enables efficient heat dissipation. SiP (System in Package) integrates multiple functionalities into one module. PBGA/CSP (Plastic Ball Grid Array/Chip Scale Package) offers reliable connection methods. BOC (Borderless Chip) and FMC (Flip Molding Chip) enhance performance and miniaturization. The automotive substrate market focuses on high reliability and performance in automotive electronics.</p></p>
