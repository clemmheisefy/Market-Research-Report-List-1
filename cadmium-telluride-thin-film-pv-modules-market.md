<p><p>Cadmium Telluride (CdTe) thin film photovoltaic (PV) modules are produced using various methods, primarily chemical bath deposition (CBD) and near-space sublimation. CBD involves immersing substrates in a solution to facilitate CdTe film formation, offering uniform coatings and cost-effectiveness. The near-space sublimation method utilizes vapor deposition from solid CdTe to create thin films in a high-temperature environment, enhancing material purity and efficiency. Other market methods may include spray deposition and screen printing, emphasizing versatility in production techniques for CdTe PV modules.</p></p>
