<p><p>The cell line generation market includes various types tailored for specific research needs. Knockout cell lines are created by disabling genes to study loss-of-function effects. Overexpression cell lines involve the increased expression of target genes, facilitating gain-of-function studies. Inducible expression cell lines allow for controlled gene expression, providing flexibility in experimentation. Knockdown cell lines utilize technologies like siRNA to reduce gene expression. Knockin cell lines incorporate specific gene modifications, while "Others" encompasses additional specialized cell lines used in diverse applications.</p></p>
