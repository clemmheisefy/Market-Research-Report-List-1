<p><ul><li>Internal Climbing Crane</li><li>External Climbing Crane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The climbing tower crane market is divided into two main types: internal climbing cranes and external climbing cranes. Internal climbing cranes are designed to operate within the structure of a building, utilizing the core for stability and minimizing the crane's external footprint. In contrast, external climbing cranes are erected outside the building and can reach higher elevations, making them suitable for tall constructions. Both types optimize vertical construction efficiency, catering to different project needs and site constraints.</p></p>
