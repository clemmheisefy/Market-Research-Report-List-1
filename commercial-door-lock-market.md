<p><p>The commercial door lock market comprises two main types: mechanical locks and electronic locks. Mechanical locks operate using traditional key-and-pin systems or deadbolts, offering durability and simplicity. They are often preferred for their reliability and lower upfront costs. In contrast, electronic locks utilize keypads, biometrics, or smart technology, providing enhanced security features and remote access capabilities. While they typically involve higher installation costs, electronic locks appeal to businesses seeking advanced security solutions and convenience in managing access control.</p></p>
