<p><ul><li>Single Disc Couplings</li><li>Double Disc Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disc couplings market is primarily segmented into single disc couplings and double disc couplings. Single disc couplings consist of a single flexible disc that accommodates misalignment and absorbs shock, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, double disc couplings feature two flexible discs connected by a spacer, providing greater torsional flexibility and alignment compensation. Both types are valued for their durability, efficiency, and ability to handle high torque loads in machinery and drive systems.</p></p>
