<p><p>The sanitary thermowells market comprises three primary types: tapered, straight, and stepped thermowells. Tapered thermowells feature a conical design that minimizes flow disturbances and enhances responsiveness to temperature changes, making them ideal for applications with variable flow rates. Straight thermowells provide a consistent diameter, suited for most standard measurements and easy installation. Stepped thermowells incorporate distinct diameters along their length, allowing for flexibility in processing environments. Each type is tailored for specific sanitary applications, ensuring accurate temperature monitoring in hygiene-critical settings.</p></p>
