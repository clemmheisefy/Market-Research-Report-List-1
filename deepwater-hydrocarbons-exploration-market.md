<p><p>Deepwater hydrocarbons exploration involves various technologies for extracting oil and gas from underwater reserves. Subsea systems are advanced structures installed on the sea floor, enabling direct access to reservoirs. Fixed platform rigs are anchored to the seabed, suitable for shallow to moderate depths. Jack-up rigs are mobile platforms that can be elevated above water, making them ideal for shallow waters. Spar platforms are tethered floating structures, optimized for deepwater conditions, while compliant tower rigs utilize flexible towers to withstand harsh ocean environments.</p></p>
