<p><p>Cubic screw jacks are mechanical devices used for lifting, lowering, and positioning loads with precision. The market is divided into two main types: translating screw and rotating screw. Translating screw jacks utilize linear movement to raise or lower loads vertically, making them ideal for applications requiring straight-line motion. In contrast, rotating screw jacks employ a rotating mechanism to achieve similar results, offering smoother operation for horizontal or angular adjustments. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
