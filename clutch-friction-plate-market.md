<p><ul><li>Dry Friction Plate</li><li>Wet Friction Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clutch friction plate market features two primary types: dry friction plates and wet friction plates. Dry friction plates operate in a friction-based system, typically found in manual transmissions, utilizing air for cooling, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Conversely, wet friction plates are submerged in oil, reducing wear and heat generation, which enhances durability and efficiency, particularly in automatic transmissions. Both types serve essential roles in transferring power and ensuring smooth vehicle operation, catering to different driving conditions and preferences.</p></p>
