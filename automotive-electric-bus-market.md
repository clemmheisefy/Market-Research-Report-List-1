<p><p>The automotive electric bus market comprises three primary types: Battery Electric Buses (BEBs), which rely solely on electric batteries for power and feature zero emissions; Plug-in Hybrid Electric Buses (PHEBs), which combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor and can be charged from an external power source; and Fuel Cell Electric Buses (FCEBs), which generate electricity through hydrogen fuel cells, emitting only water vapor. Each type offers distinct advantages in sustainability, operational range, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
