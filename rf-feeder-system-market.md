<p><ul><li>Coax or Coaxial Cable</li><li>Open Wire or Twin Feeder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF feeder system market primarily features two types: Coaxial Cable and Open Wire or Twin Feeder. Coaxial cables consist of a central conductor, insulation, and shielding, ideal for minimizing signal loss over long distances and providing durability. In contrast, Open Wire or Twin Feeders use parallel wires, offering lower loss at high frequencies and flexibility in installation. Both types serve critical roles in transmitting radio frequency signals in various applications, including telecommunications, broadcasting, and wireless communications.</p></p>
