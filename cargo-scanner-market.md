<p><p>The cargo scanner market comprises various technologies for scanning and inspecting cargo. Gamma-ray radiography technology utilizes high-energy gamma rays to penetrate dense materials, providing detailed images of cargo contents. X-ray radiography technology employs lower energy X-rays, allowing for quick inspections of containers while maintaining safety. Muon tomography technology relies on cosmic muons, offering a non-invasive method to view cargo without significant radiation exposure. Each technology serves distinct needs for security, customs inspection, and loss prevention in goods transportation.</p></p>
