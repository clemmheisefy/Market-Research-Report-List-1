<p><p>The bitumen market comprises three primary types: natural bitumen, petroleum bitumen, and coal-tar pitch. Natural bitumen, found in deposits, is used mainly in road construction and waterproofing. Petroleum bitumen, derived from crude oil refining, is widely utilized in road surfacing, roofing, and insulation due to its favorable adhesive properties. Coal-tar pitch, a byproduct of coal carbonization, is primarily used in aluminum production, road construction, and roofing, owing to its durability and water-resistant characteristics. Each type serves distinct industrial applications.</p></p>
