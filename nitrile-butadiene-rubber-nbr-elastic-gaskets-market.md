<p><ul><li>Saddle</li><li>Waveform</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) elastic gaskets are primarily categorized into saddle and waveform types. Saddle gaskets have a distinct U-shape, providing a tight seal in applications requiring high compression and stability. Conversely, waveform gaskets feature undulating surfaces, enhancing flexibility and adaptability to uneven surfaces while ensuring a reliable seal. Both types are widely used in automotive, industrial, and electrical applications due to their excellent resistance to oils, fuels, and abrasion, making them essential for maintaining leak-proof environments.</p></p>
