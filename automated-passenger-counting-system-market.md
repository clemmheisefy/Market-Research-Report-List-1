<p><p>The Automated Passenger Counting System market consists of various technologies for tracking passenger numbers. Infrared sensors use heat detection to count individuals, making them efficient for low-light environments. Stereoscopic vision employs dual cameras to analyze depth and accurately count passengers. Time-of-Flight systems measure the time it takes for light pulses to return, providing precise data even in crowded spaces. IP cameras leverage advanced image processing and network capabilities for real-time monitoring and reporting, enhancing overall passenger management efficiency.</p></p>
