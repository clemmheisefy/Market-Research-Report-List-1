<p><strong>The Hot-Air Coffee Roasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Drum Roasters</li><li>Perforated Drum Roasters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot-Air Coffee Roasters can be categorized into two main market types: Solid Drum Roasters and Perforated Drum Roasters. Solid Drum Roasters feature a continuous drum that retains heat efficiently, producing uniform roasts, ideal for small to medium-scale operations. In contrast, Perforated Drum Roasters incorporate airflow through perforations, enhancing heat distribution and allowing for quicker roasting times. This enables better control over the roasting profile, appealing to specialty coffee producers seeking precise flavors and consistency in their products.</p></p>
