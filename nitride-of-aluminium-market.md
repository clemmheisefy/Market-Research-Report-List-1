<p><p>The Aluminum Nitride (AlN) market is primarily shaped by three production methods: Direct Nitridation, Carbothermal Reduction, and Nitridation. The Direct Nitridation method involves heating aluminum in a nitrogen atmosphere, yielding high purity AlN. Carbothermal Reduction utilizes carbon to reduce aluminum oxide with nitrogen, producing AlN along with carbon oxides. Lastly, the Nitridation method involves reacting aluminum with nitrogen at elevated temperatures, often in the presence of other materials, enhancing its properties for various industrial applications.</p></p>
