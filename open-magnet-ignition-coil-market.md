<p><p>The Open-magnet Ignition Coil Market comprises two main types: Single Oscillator and Dual Oscillator. Single Oscillator coils utilize a single resonant circuit to generate high-voltage pulses, making them simpler and cost-effective for basic ignition systems. In contrast, Dual Oscillator coils incorporate two oscillating circuits that enhance performance, providing better efficiency and reliability in high-demand applications. These distinctions cater to varying vehicle and machinery requirements, influencing their adoption in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
