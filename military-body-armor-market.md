<p><ul><li>Hard Armor</li><li>Soft Armor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military body armor market is primarily divided into two types: hard armor and soft armor. Hard armor typically consists of rigid materials like ceramic or steel plates, designed to provide protection against high-caliber rounds and shrapnel, making it suitable for high-risk combat situations. Soft armor, on the other hand, is made from flexible materials such as Kevlar, offering lighter, more comfortable protection against lower-caliber threats, often used in situations where mobility is crucial and ballistic protection is still needed.</p></p>
