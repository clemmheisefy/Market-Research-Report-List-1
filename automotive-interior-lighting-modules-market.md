<p><ul><li>Halogen Lighting</li><li>HID Lighting</li><li>LED Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive interior lighting modules market comprises three main types: halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting. Halogen lighting is traditional, offering warm illumination but low energy efficiency. HID lighting provides brighter light with improved visibility and efficiency, making it popular for upscale vehicles. LED lighting is increasingly favored due to its longevity, low power consumption, and design flexibility, enabling innovative interior lighting solutions. This market is driven by advancements in technology and increasing consumer demands for enhanced vehicle aesthetics.</p></p>
