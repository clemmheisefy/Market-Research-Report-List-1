<p><p>The Silver Powder for Conductive Coating market comprises two main types: Flake Silver Powder and Spherical Silver Powder. Flake Silver Powder is characterized by its thin, flat particles, which offer a larger surface area for better conductivity and adhesion in coatings. In contrast, Spherical Silver Powder features rounded particles that provide enhanced flowability and uniform dispersion, improving coating properties. Both types are crucial in electronics, automotive, and aerospace industries for enhancing electrical conductivity in various applications, including printed circuit boards and sensors.</p></p>
