<p><p>The High Pressure Centrifugal Fan market can be segmented into two main types based on air intake design: single-side air intake and bilateral air intake. Single-side air intake fans draw air from one side, optimizing space and simplifying installation, making them suitable for compact applications. In contrast, bilateral air intake fans draw air from both sides, enhancing airflow efficiency and performance, especially in larger systems where balanced air distribution is crucial. Both types cater to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
