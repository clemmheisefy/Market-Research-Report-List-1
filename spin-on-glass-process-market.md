<p><p>The Spin-on Glass Process market is primarily divided into two types: Hot-Temperature Spin Process and Normal Spin Process. The Hot-Temperature Spin Process involves applying materials at elevated temperatures, enhancing film properties, and improving adhesion on substrates. In contrast, the Normal Spin Process operates at lower temperatures, focusing on achieving uniform coatings for various electronic applications. Both methods serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, particularly for dielectrics and interlayer dielectrics, impacting device performance and reliability.</p></p>
