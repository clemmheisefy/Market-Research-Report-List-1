<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Powder market is categorized into three main types based on production methods: Direct Process, Indirect Process, and Wet Chemical Process. The Direct Process involves the oxidation of zinc metal at high temperatures, producing a coarse powder. The Indirect Process utilizes zinc oxide derived from other compounds, offering a finer product. The Wet Chemical Process combines zinc salts with alkalis, resulting in high-purity zinc oxide. Each method influences the powder's characteristics, applications, and cost-effectiveness in various industries.</p></p>
