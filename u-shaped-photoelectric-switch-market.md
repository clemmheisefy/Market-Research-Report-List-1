<p><p>U-shaped photoelectric switches are categorized into three main types: U-K, U-T, and U-L. U-K switches feature a compact design ideal for close-range sensing applications. U-T switches are designed for through-beam setups, offering extended sensing distance and stability for larger areas. U-L switches provide enhanced versatility with adjustable sensing distances, suitable for varied industrial applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs in automation, ensuring accuracy and efficiency in detecting objects within designated spaces.</p></p>
