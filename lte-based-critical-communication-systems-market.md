<p><p>LTE-based critical communication systems encompass two main market types: analog and digital. Analog systems primarily focus on traditional voice communication, often relying on narrowband channels. They offer simplicity and reliability but lack advanced features. In contrast, digital systems leverage LTE technology to provide enhanced capabilities, including high-definition voice, data transmission, and real-time multimedia sharing. This digital approach supports better interoperability, scalability, and resilience, making it the preferred choice for mission-critical applications in sectors such as public safety and emergency services.</p></p>
