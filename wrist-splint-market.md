<p><strong>The Wrist Splint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volar Splint</li><li>Thumb Spica Splint</li><li>Sugar Tong Splint</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wrist splint market encompasses various types designed for specific injuries and conditions. Volar splints immobilize the wrist and provide support while allowing thumb movement. Thumb spica splints focus on immobilizing the thumb and wrist, commonly used for thumb injuries. Sugar tong splints extend from the forearm to the hand, offering comprehensive immobilization for wrist fractures. Additionally, "others" include diverse splints like dorsal and ulnar splints, tailored to individual needs, injuries, and recovery processes.</p></p>
