<p><ul><li>Analog Microphone</li><li>Digital Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive MEMS Microphone market comprises two main types: analog microphones and digital microphones. Analog microphones output continuous signal levels, making them simple and cost-effective, but they may be more sensitive to noise. In contrast, digital microphones convert sound into digital signals, providing better noise immunity and enhanced audio quality. These characteristics make digital microphones increasingly popular in automotive applications, where clarity and reliability are crucial for systems like voice recognition, hands-free communication, and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
