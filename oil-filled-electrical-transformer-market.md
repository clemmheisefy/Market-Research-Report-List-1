<p><p>The oil-filled electrical transformer market is divided primarily into two types: closed transformers and shell transformers. Closed transformers feature an enclosed design that provides protection and insulation, making them suitable for urban areas with limited space. Shell transformers, on the other hand, have a more exposed structure and typically include a shell that surrounds the core and windings, offering enhanced efficiency and thermal performance. Both types utilize oil for cooling and insulation, crucial for their operational efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
