<p><p>The automotive glass market for heavy commercial vehicles primarily includes tempered and laminated glass, along with other types. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, making it ideal for side and rear windows. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers bonded with a plastic interlayer, providing greater impact resistance and minimizing shattering, which is essential for windshields. Other types may include acoustic or tinted glass, catering to specific performance, safety, and aesthetic requirements in heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
